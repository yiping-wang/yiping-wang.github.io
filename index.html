Currently, I am a Master of Mathematics student in the Cheriton School of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo, and I am a Vector Scholarship in Artificial Intelligence recipient 2021-22. Previously, I received an Honours Bachelor of Science with Distinction from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Victoria, and I was the class of 2021 W.E. Cowie Innovation Award recipient and a Jamie Cassels Undergraduate Research Award recipient 2020-21.

My interests and enthusiasm are for Artificial Intelligence for Healthcare. I am exploring how to use medical data and machine learning to help doctors make diagnostic decisions. I am broadly keen on causal representation learning, un-/weakly-/self-supervised learning for Computer Vision to improve cancer detection and classification, lesion segmentation and quantification in all types of imaging modalities. I am fascinated by Natural Language Processing for analyzing and interpreting genomics, proteomics, and clinical reports, and their linkages between images to predict patient outcomes.
